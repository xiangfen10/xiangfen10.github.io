Chuangpu pipe mould company profile


Shanxi Chuangpu pipe mould Co., Ltd., from the domestic large mould factory,It is located in "The hometown of mechanical processing"Houma City, Shanxi Province.which is a professional manufacturer of pipe moulds. It mainly produces large-scale straight pipe moulds, Type pipe and other deep-hole processing products, and has successfully provided qualified products for Hebei Xinxing, Anyang Yongtong and other large domestic mould pipe groups. The products are distributed in more than 30 provinces and cities across the country, with a domestic market share of 10%. The company has the right to self-export, and also sells to the United States, Russia, India, South Korea, Taiwan and other countries and regions.

 

The company has the processing of "centrifugal mould pipe" moulds,Special equipment for the processing of special steel straight pipe moulds. Including deep hole drilling and boring machine T2132, CNC deep hole boring machine T2250, T2280 (boring diameter up to 1200mm, boring depth 8000mm), a single deep hole boring machine equipment length of more than 25 meters. CNC lathes CK61100, CK61125, CK61180, CQ61180 (maximum turning diameter 1800mm, processing length 9000mm), etc.

 

In order to better serve the mould pipe enterprises, the company hires the industry manufacturing and industry to use the“double consultant”chief engineer-level professional and technical personnel to check, both in the pipe mould processing and the use of pipe mould are using international leading technology. Skilled workers mainly employ arsenal retirees to ensure the “military quality”of the product.

 

The pipes are all from large state-owned special steel enterprises，at least 55,000 tons of dedicated forging water presses, to ensure that there are no forging defects inside the pipe mould blank. 15m~30m professional pit heat treatment furnace is adopted, and non-welding heat treatment method is adopted. Strict control except for basic material requirements trace elements such as O,Vi, Ti are used in the melting and moulding process to ensure the performance of the mould and provideThermal fatigue in extubation use and intrinsic assurance of extubation.

 

The company takes the opportunity of "improving" management ,stimulate the enthusiasm of employees for invent and innovate.Now it has more than 20 patents. Including the patented technology of automatic shrinking knife taper hole processing of floating boring, the patented technology of automatic recording of the number of pipe dials and automatic monitoring of circulating water temperature of circulating water, the patented technology of vibration to eliminate the stress generated by processing, the patented technology of ultrasonic flaw detection of empty pipes of the pipe mould, the patented technology of accurate alignment of the annular center frame of the small-diameter pipe mould, and the patented technology of the boring machine self-boring kit to ensure the accuracy of the equipment.Patented technology for calculating the wall thickness difference of pipe mould socket, special patented technology for anti-tensile crack surfacing welding for submerged arc welding, patented technology for automatic processing of CNC pipe head, etc.Ensure that there are no internal defects in the pipe mould blank, the internal hole is fine boring and the natural taper of the inner hole of the pipe mould is uniform and the straightness is perfect.When moulding pipes, the use environment of the pipe mould is newly detected, which can greatly improve the service life of the pipe mould.

 

The company adheres to the business philosophy of "innovation and inclusiveness", adheres to the leading quality, brand assurance,Cultivate technology pioneers and create technological leadership.
