Company Profile


Shanxi Chuangpu Pipe Mould Co., Ltd. , located in Houma City, Shanxi Province of China, is a professional production of ductile iron pipe mould and cast iron drain pipe mould enterprise. The annual processing capacity of forgings and heat-treated parts is more than 10000 tons and large ductile iron pipe mould (DN100~1200*6000mm) is more than 1000 pieces.

 

The company adopts new large-scale special equipment. Including T2250, T2280 CNC deep hole boring machine (boring diameter up to 1200mm, boring depth up to 8000mm), the length of a single deep hole boring machine is more than 25m. CNC lathe CKJ61125, CKJ61180(maximum rotary diameter of 1800mm, processing length of 9000mm); Professional and advanced equipment is in the domestic leading level.

 

The company employs chief professional and technical personnel of the industry manufacturing and use as "double consultant" to check on strictly, whether it is processing or use of the pipe mould, it has reached international advanced technology. The main technical personnel are employed from the retirees of the military factory to ensure the "military quality" of the products.

 

All of our cooperative suppliers are large state-owned enterprises, equipped with 55,000 tons of special hydraulic press and 15-30 meters of professional wellhead heat treatment furnace, as well as adopted non-welding heat treatment mode. The quality of the material determines the mechanical properties of the products, thereby ensuring the resistance of thermal fatigue and the number of pull-out pipes.

 

In order to ensure the uniformity and straightness of the fine boring of the inner hole and step hole of the ductile iron pipe die, as well as the dimensional accuracy and flatness of the R-plane and inclined plane of the socket of the ductile iron pipe die, the production and processing technology of the hopper knife deep hole step hole and the processing technology of the digital control pipe head are adopted.

 

Our company adhering to the "innovation, win-win" business philosophy, adhere to the quality priority, brand assurance, create the leading technology, based on China, to the world.
