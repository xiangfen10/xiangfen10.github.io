About us

Located in the "mechanical processing city" Houma City, Shanxi Province, specializing in the production of ductile iron pipe mold

  • The company employs the industry manufacturing and industry use "double consultant" chief engineer level of professional and technical personnel to check the pass, in the pipe mold processing and the use of the pipe mold has the international leading technology level. The main technical workers employ the internal retirees of the arms factory to ensure the "military quality" of the products.

  • The company uses new large-scale special equipment. Including CNC deep hole boring machine T2250, T2280 (boring diameter up to 1200mm, boring depth of 8000mm), a single deep hole boring machine length of more than 25 meters. CNC lathe CKJ61125, CKJ61180 (maximum rotary diameter of 1800mm, processing length of 9000mm); The dedicated and advanced equipment belongs to the domestic leading level.


  • The cooperative supplier is a large state-owned enterprise, equipped with 55,000 tons of special hydraulic press and 15 ~30 meters of well heat treatment furnace, using non-welding heat treatment mode. The material quality determines the mechanical properties of the product, which provides the internal guarantee for the thermal fatigue and the number of tube pulling in use.

  • The production and processing adopt automatic skip deep hole boring step hole processing technology, and numerical control pipe head processing machine technology, to ensure the fine boring of the inner hole of the ductile iron pipe mold and the step hole progressive uniformity and straightness, to ensure the dimensional accuracy and smoothness of the R surface and inclined surface of the socket of the ductile iron pipe mold.
